--- Part Two ---

You are now ready for fruit list.

At the beginning of fruit list, you start with a fruit sum of 0. On each turn, the player moves down the list, and the value is added to your fruit sum.

When your fruit sum is a multiplier of 10, an elimination occurs, and your fruit sum is divided by 10. Elimination will only happen once per turn. If your fruit sum is 100, it will become 10, and will not be further divided to 1.

Your final score is obtained by multiplying the number of elimination and your final fruit sum together.

Consider the example above:

Turn 1:

Sum = 1 (+1), Elimination = 0

Turn 2:

Sum = 5 (+4), Elimination = 0

Turn 3:

Sum = 7 (+2), Elimination = 0

Turn 4:

Sum = 10 (+3), Elimination = 0

Because the sum is a multiple of 10, an elimination occurs:

Sum = 1, Elimination = 1 (+1)

Turn 5:

Sum = 4 (+3), Elimination = 1

...

Turn 11:

Sum = 18 (+1), Elimination = 2

Therefore, the final score is 18 * 2 = 36.
