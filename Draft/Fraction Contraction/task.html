Once again, check for the product of the sum of the different parts in the reduced fractions. There are three parts in these fractions: a whole part, a numerator and a denominator. Fractions smaller than 1 (like 2 / 5) do not have a whole part, and whole numbers (like 1) do not have a numerator or a denominator. In this example, the sum of the whole parts is 10, the sum of the numerators is 19 and the sum of the denominators is 35, so the answer to the homework is 10 * 19 * 35 = 6650.
